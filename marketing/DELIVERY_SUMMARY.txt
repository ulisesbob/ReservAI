RISKS & MITIGATION
=============================================================================

RISK 1: Low conversion (2% instead of 5%)
MITIGATION: Increase messaging specificity, do more 1:1 demo calls, test different copy

RISK 2: High churn (10%+ instead of 5%)
MITIGATION: Improve onboarding, add customer success calls, add features based on feedback

RISK 3: WhatsApp API instability
MITIGATION: Use managed provider (Twilio, Messagebird) instead of direct API

RISK 4: Competitor copies WhatsApp bot
MITIGATION: Build community/network effects, iterate fast (weekly features), focus on service

RISK 5: Influencer campaign flops
MITIGATION: Pivot to direct sales, use LinkedIn B2B ads instead

RISK 6: Ads have poor ROAS
MITIGATION: Pause ads, focus on founder-led sales + referrals (slower but more profitable)
