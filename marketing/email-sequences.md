## EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### Subject Line Strategy
- Curiosity: "¿Viste esto en tu dashboard?"
- Urgency: "Tu trial termina hoy"
- Benefit: "Reducir no-shows 40%"
- Personalization: [Nombre] or [Restaurante]
- Numbers: "50 restaurantes", "40% reducción"

### Copy Best Practices
- Start with specific number or fact (not generic)
- Speak to real problem (not feature)
